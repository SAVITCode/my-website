About This Project

The Origin

SavitCode started as a personal experiment to turn trading ideas into real tools. I wanted something that bridges the gap between charting and automation — and make it simple enough for traders who aren't coders.

The Vision

From Pine Script to Python, from alerts to automation — this project is built to empower creators and traders alike. We're not here to promise overnight success or magic formulas.
"No hype. Just clean, useful tools."

What We Build

Smart Alerts

Pine Script indicators that actually matter. Built for precision, not noise.

Custom Tools

Python scripts and automation to support your analysis workflow.

Practical Solutions

Real problems, real solutions. No overcomplicated nonsense.

The Philosophy

Technology should amplify your skills, not replace your judgment. Every tool we create is designed to give you better information, faster insights, and cleaner execution. The market is complex enough — your tools shouldn't be.
